# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to create a welcoming and harassment-free community for everyone.
We value respect, inclusivity, and constructive collaboration.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior include:

* Being respectful and kind.
* Accepting feedback gracefully.
* Focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

* Harassment, personal attacks, or threats.
* Publishing private information without permission.
* Disruptive or offensive behavior in community spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to:
**[juliocmbuenotrabalho@outlook.com](mailto:juliocmbuenotrabalho@outlook.com)**.
All reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially.

## Enforcement

Community leaders may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
